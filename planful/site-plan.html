Overview

purpose

Helping to plan out dates and/or family outings on a budget. The user will put in a mile radius and a price limit for their outing, the website would then help them find restaurants, movies, events, activities, discounts and deals going on within that price point and area. This is a great way to explore your own city and help you try new places, and find out about current and upcoming events going on around them. We will be able to help people plan fun and different activities without breaking the bank. Who wouldn't want to just enjoy a special outing without the stress of planning and worrying about a budget? This website will take care of that!

audience

for couples of all ages, married or dating, and families of all sizes.

Content

Home page

Planful is a unique and easy way to plan dates and family outings on a budget in your area! you set the location boundaries and price limit and we take care of the rest. With Planful you can plan ahead or use us as a last minute outing planner. We show you all of the restaurants, movies, concerts, museums, parks, and so much more happening right in the area you've selected with the price point you choose. Whether you're trying to plan a fun date, or a special family activity, Planful understands the importance of making time for those you care about most, without breaking the bank. Find hidden gems that have been right around the corner like the "ma and pa" diner that's soon to be your favorite new spot! or check the Planful Calendar that has upcoming events in the area you've selected with tickets in your price range. With Planful you don't have to feel like fun new experiences are out of reach, and knowing where to look can seem like a daunting task with so many options. But with Planful we bring them to you within your area and price point. Mindful Planning. Planful.

How It Works

So, how does it work? first Planful will help narrow down what you're looking to do. Group (family) outing? or plan a date? then it will ask you when are you planning to out: ASAP? or "Choose a future date"? after that it will give you select your price point and your mile radius to travel. TA-DA! That's it! Planful does the rest. We'll give all different options to choose from, from there you can filter your search by choosing to veiw only restaurants, movies, parks, and so much more! Planning a fun and unique outing really can be just that simple! Let Planful plan your next outing and see how simple it can really be. Want to add multiple stops to your outing and stay within your budget? Planful's got you covered! Want find discounts and promo codes for the outings you want to do? Planful will help find those for you! Whether you're planning ahead or last minute- Planful's got the deal that's right for you.

Why Choose Planful?

Planful is the new mindful planning website, and we understand that times are tough, money is tight, and gas is expensive. Dates and family outings almost seem like a thing of the past. But not with Planful. Whether you're a family of 10+ or trying to go on a first date, Planful makes it easy and fun by giving you options you can feel comfortable with the people you care about the most. Extra tight budget? Planful offers free and low cost options. You might be surprised to know about all the events and specials going on in your town. Planful helps bring the community together by showing you small businesses owned by the people around you. Planful helps to guide you to try new places and explore the community around you. Learn about grand openings, business anniversary deals, business birthday deals, and so much more! Planful knows how important it is to stay in budget and never shows you offers outside of the limit that you give us. We work with you to find something special right at or under your budget. And we do our best to include all costs associated with the chosen activity. Some additional charges and fees may apply, Planful encourages users to review and help keep information updated and as accurate as possible to help give everyone the best experience possible.
