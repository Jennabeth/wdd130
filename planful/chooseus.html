Why Choose Planful?

Planful is the new mindful planning website, and we understand that times are tough, money is tight, and gas is expensive. Dates and family outings almost seem like a thing of the past. But not with Planful. Whether you're a family of 10+ or trying to go on a first date, Planful makes it easy and fun by giving you options you can feel comfortable with the people you care about the most.

Extra tight budget? Planful offers free and low cost options. You might be surprised to know about all the events and specials going on in your town. Planful helps bring the community together by showing you small businesses owned by the people around you. Planful helps to guide you to try new places and explore the community around you. Learn about grand openings, business anniversary deals, business birthday deals, and so much more!

Planful knows how important it is to stay in budget and never shows you offers outside of the limit that you give us. We work with you to find something special right at or under your budget. And we do our best to include all costs associated with the chosen activity. Some additional charges and fees may apply, Planful encourages users to review and help keep information updated and as accurate as possible to help give everyone the best experience possible.
